iREAP
Rural entrepreneurship and agribusiness preincubation
i REAP (rural entrepreneurship and agribusiness preincubation) program aims to identify and promote early stage startups in the agro based economy and provide them quality support in terms of mentorship, access to labs, technology credits, funding, industry partnerships and workspaces to ensure their growth as an enterprise
